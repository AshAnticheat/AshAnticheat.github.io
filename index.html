About

What is Ash Anticheat?

Ash is an upcomming Anticheat with the goal of being optimized and an advanced anticheat.

Ash currently contains 7 checks which analyze players for suspicious combat, interaction, movement, and malicious exploitation within the protocol. Ash's checks will only observe the players' actions and collect statistics based on the observations. No checks test for cheats by interrupting the game experience, such as spawning NPCs or applying knock-back.

Features

Asynchronous

Most of the Ash plugin runs on other threads which will drastically reduce lag when using Ash. We also have an adaptating thread system to fit your server's capabilities.

Dependency Free

Unlike many other anticheat, Ash runs on NMS and no ProtocolLib is currently required.

Simple, but powerful checks

Our current checks are simple, however they are almost impossible to false flag for the most part.

Movement Checking

Currently, Ash is only focused on movement cheats but will be focused on more soon.

Customizable Configuration

In Ash, you can customize anything! From the amounts of violations to the check settings.

Cheats Detected

Fight Critical's

Verifies movement when attempting to land critical hits.

Fly

Verifies that a player falls when not standing on anything.

Speed (Movement)

Verifies horizontal movement velocity.

BadPackets

Analyzes for badly fabricated packets.

Ground Spoof

Validates the integrity of the "ground" flag in PacketPlayInFlying packets.
